BODHI ENGINEERING PRIVATE LIMITED

BODHI ENGINEERING PRIVATE LIMITED is a provider of tower and related infrastructure and on a consolidated basis, we are one of the largest tower infrastructure providers in Bihar & Jharkhand. Not only did we pioneer the tower infrastructure sharing concept in India, but are also today the leaders with over 1000+ Towers across the circles we operate in, with some of them in the remotest and tough terrains. We also pioneered the concept of environment-friendly Towers or ‘GreenTowers’ and energy-efficient methods for maintenance of these towers. Wireless technology connects people, devices, and networks throughout the world. We help make that possible. Our multi-tenant leasing model offers communications sites that bridge the communication gap, keeping property owners. We are a company committed to developing shared success.
